# IL-LABOUR-ESI-COMPLIANCE

## Protocol ID
IL-LABOUR-ESI-COMPLIANCE

## Classification
- **Domain**: Labour Law

- **Type**: Operational Protocol

- **Version**: 1.0.0

## Purpose
This protocol provides comprehensive guidance on compliance with the Employees' State Insurance Act 1948, covering registration, contribution calculations, medical benefits, cash benefits (sickness, maternity, disability, dependents), claims procedures, inspections, and penalties.

---

## Core Framework

### 1. APPLICABILITY (Section 1, 2)

#### 1.1 Coverage
```
ESI (EMPLOYEES' STATE INSURANCE) ACT 1948:

MANDATORY FOR:
1. Factories using power:
   - ≥10 employees (any day in preceding 12 months)

2. Factories not using power:
   - ≥20 employees (any day)

3. Shops, establishments, restaurants, hotels, cinemas, road transport:
   - As notified by State Government (usually ≥10 employees)

4. Educational, medical institutions (private):
   - As notified (varies by state)

WAGE LIMIT (Section 2(9)):
Applies to employees earning ≤₹21,000/month

CALCULATION:
Gross wages (Basic + DA + HRA + All allowances + Bonus)
If Gross >₹21,000/month → NOT covered under ESI
If Gross ≤₹21,000/month → MANDATORY coverage

EXAMPLE:
Employee A: Basic ₹15,000 + DA ₹3,000 + HRA ₹2,500 = ₹20,500 (ESI applicable)
Employee B: Basic ₹18,000 + DA ₹5,000 + HRA ₹3,000 = ₹26,000 (ESI NOT applicable)

EMPLOYEE DEFINITION:
Person employed for wages (skilled, unskilled, manual, clerical, supervisory)
Excludes: Managerial, administrative staff (if wage >₹21,000)

ONCE COVERED, ALWAYS COVERED (till separation):
If employee's wage increases beyond ₹21,000 after joining:
→ ESI coverage continues (cannot drop mid-employment)
Exception: If wage >₹21,000 at joining → Never covered
```

### 2. REGISTRATION (Section 2A, 40)

#### 2.1 Employer Registration
```
MANDATORY (Section 2A):
Every employer (factory/establishment) covered by ESI Act must register

AUTHORITY:
ESIC (Employees' State Insurance Corporation) - Regional Office

PROCEDURE:

STEP 1: APPLICATION (online via ESIC portal www.esic.in)
Details:
- Employer name, address, type of establishment
- PAN, GST, Shops & Establishments registration (if applicable)
- Nature of business/manufacturing
- Number of employees (total, ESI-covered)
- Date of coverage (when ≥10/20 employees first employed)

STEP 2: DOCUMENTS TO UPLOAD
- PAN card (establishment)
- Cancelled cheque (employer's bank account for contribution deposits)
- Shops & Establishments certificate OR Factories Act registration
- List of employees (ESI-eligible, with Aadhaar, bank details)

STEP 3: CODE NUMBER ALLOTTED
ESIC allots 17-digit Code Number (unique employer ID)
Format: [State Code]-[Regional Office]-[Sub-regional]-[Branch]-[Employer Serial]

STEP 4: REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE
Downloadable from portal (PDF)
Display at establishment (prominently, accessible to employees)

TIMELINE:
Register within 15 days of Act becoming applicable

PENALTY FOR NON-REGISTRATION (Section 85):
Imprisonment up to 3 years + Fine up to ₹10,000
(Serious offense, strictly enforced)
```

#### 2.2 Employee Registration & Insurance Number
```
IP NUMBER (Insured Person Number):
Unique 17-digit number for each ESI-covered employee
Format: [Employer Code]-[Employee Serial Number]

ISSUANCE:
Employer registers employee on ESIC portal
Within 10 days of joining (if ESI-applicable)
Upload: Name, Father's name, DOB, Aadhaar, Bank account, Family details

FAMILY DETAILS (for medical benefits):
- Spouse
- Children (up to 2, dependent)
- Parents (if dependent, living with employee)

E-PEHCHAN CARD:
Issued to employee + family members
Biometric card (photo, fingerprint)
Used at ESIC hospitals/dispensaries for treatment

TEMPORARY IP NUMBER:
For new employees (till permanent IP generated)
Employer issues (on letterhead), valid for 3 months
Employee can avail treatment using temporary IP

PORTABILITY:
IP number is portable (if employee changes jobs, same IP number continues)
New employer links existing IP to their Code Number
```

### 3. CONTRIBUTIONS (Section 38, 39, 44)

#### 3.1 Contribution Rates
```
EMPLOYEE CONTRIBUTION: 0.75% of wages
EMPLOYER CONTRIBUTION: 3.25% of wages
TOTAL: 4% of wages

WAGES DEFINITION (for contribution calculation):
All remuneration paid/payable in cash (Basic + DA + HRA + Allowances + Overtime + Bonus)
Excludes: Reimbursements (travel, medical bills, conveyance if actual bills submitted)

CALCULATION EXAMPLE:
Employee wages: ₹18,000/month (Basic ₹12,000 + DA ₹3,000 + HRA ₹3,000)

Employee contribution: ₹18,000 × 0.75% = ₹135
Employer contribution: ₹18,000 × 3.25% = ₹585
Total contribution: ₹720/month

Annual: ₹720 × 12 = ₹8,640
```

#### 3.2 Contribution Payment
```
CHALLAN GENERATION:
Online via ESIC portal (monthly)
Due date: 15th of following month

PAYMENT MODE:
- Net banking (NEFT, RTGS from employer's bank account)
- Challan reference number generated after payment

TIMELINE:
Contribution for January 2025 → Pay by 15th February 2025

DELAYED PAYMENT:
- Interest: 12% p.a. (from due date till payment)
- Damages: Up to 25% of contribution amount (if delay >2 months)

EXAMPLE (Delayed Payment):
Total contribution due (Jan 2025): ₹50,000
Due date: 15th Feb 2025
Actual payment: 15th April 2025 (2 months delay)

Interest: ₹50,000 × 12% × 2/12 = ₹1,000
Damages: 25% × ₹50,000 = ₹12,500 (at ESIC's discretion)
Total: ₹50,000 + ₹1,000 + ₹12,500 = ₹63,500

CRIMINAL LIABILITY (Section 85):
Willful default (>6 months): Imprisonment up to 3 years + Fine
```

### 4. MEDICAL BENEFITS (Section 46)

#### 4.1 Coverage
```
BENEFICIARIES:
- Insured employee (IP holder)
- Spouse
- Children (up to 2, dependent, unmarried)
- Parents (if dependent on IP, living with IP)

TREATMENT:
FREE OF COST (no payment at ESIC facility)
- Outpatient care (OPD): Dispensaries, polyclinics
- Inpatient care (IPD): ESIC hospitals
- Specialist consultations
- Diagnostic tests (X-ray, blood tests, ultrasound, CT/MRI if medically necessary)
- Medicines (free from ESIC pharmacy)
- Surgery, hospitalization (all costs borne by ESIC)

FACILITIES:
1. ESIC Dispensaries (primary care):
   - General physician consultation
   - Basic medicines
   - Referral to hospital (if specialist needed)

2. ESIC Hospitals (secondary/tertiary care):
   - Multi-specialty (cardiology, orthopedics, gynecology, pediatrics)
   - 24/7 emergency
   - Surgery (general, specialized)
   - ICU, NICU facilities

3. Tie-up Hospitals (if ESIC facility not available):
   - Private hospitals empaneled by ESIC
   - Cashless treatment (ESIC pays hospital directly)
   - Emergency cases (if nearest ESIC hospital >8 km)

DENTAL, OPHTHALMIC CARE:
Covered (spectacles, dental treatment, dentures - as per ESIC norms)

CHRONIC DISEASES:
Diabetes, hypertension, cancer, kidney disease → Lifelong treatment free

MATERNITY:
Pre-natal, delivery, post-natal care (covered under medical benefit + cash maternity benefit)
```

### 5. CASH BENEFITS

#### 5.1 Sickness Benefit (Section 46)
```
ELIGIBILITY: Employee contributed for ≥78 days in preceding 6 months

RATE: 70% of average daily wages
DURATION: Up to 91 days per year (extendable to 124 days if chronic disease)

CALCULATION:
Average daily wage = Total wages in preceding 4 months ÷ days for which wages paid

EXAMPLE:
Employee sick for 15 days (certified by ESIC doctor)
Last 4 months wages: ₹72,000 (₹18,000 × 4)
Days worked in 4 months: 104 days (26 days/month × 4)
Average daily wage: ₹72,000 ÷ 104 = ₹692/day
Sickness benefit: 70% × ₹692 × 15 days = ₹7,266

CLAIM PROCEDURE:
Submit medical certificate (ESIC doctor) + claim form within 14 days of illness
Benefit paid within 30 days (directly to employee's bank account)
```

#### 5.2 Maternity Benefit (Section 50)
```
ELIGIBILITY: Woman employee contributed for ≥70 days in preceding 12 months

RATE: 100% of average daily wages (not 70%, full wages)
DURATION: 26 weeks (182 days) for first 2 children, 12 weeks for 3rd child onwards

CALCULATION:
Same average daily wage formula (total wages in preceding 4 months ÷ days worked)

EXAMPLE:
Maternity leave: 182 days
Last 4 months wages: ₹80,000
Days worked: 104 days
Average daily wage: ₹80,000 ÷ 104 = ₹769/day
Maternity benefit: 100% × ₹769 × 182 days = ₹1,39,958

MEDICAL BONUS: ₹3,500 (if no pre/post-natal care at ESIC facility)

CLAIM: Submit expected delivery date certificate, actual delivery certificate
Payment: 50% advance (6 weeks before delivery), 50% after delivery
```

## Compliance Checklist

### For Employers
- [ ] Register establishment within 15 days (if ≥10/20 employees, wages ≤₹21,000)
- [ ] Register employees within 10 days of joining (upload to ESIC portal)
- [ ] Deduct employee contribution 0.75%, add employer contribution 3.25%
- [ ] Pay contribution by 15th of following month (via ESIC portal, net banking)
- [ ] Issue E-Pehchan cards to employees + families (download from portal)
- [ ] Display ESIC registration certificate, contribution rates at establishment
- [ ] Maintain records (contribution challans, employee master list for 5 years)

### For Employees
- [ ] Verify IP number issued (within 1 month of joining)
- [ ] Download E-Pehchan card (carry to ESIC facility for treatment)
- [ ] Register family members (spouse, children, parents) for medical coverage
- [ ] Avail treatment at ESIC dispensary/hospital (free, cashless)
- [ ] File sickness benefit claim within 14 days (with ESIC doctor certificate)
- [ ] File maternity benefit claim in advance (6 weeks before expected delivery)

---

## Penalties (Section 85)
- Non-registration: Imprisonment up to 3 years + Fine ₹10,000
- Delayed contribution (>6 months): Imprisonment up to 3 years + Interest 12% + Damages 25%
- Not issuing IP number: Fine ₹5,000-₹25,000
- False claims: Imprisonment up to 1 year + Fine + Refund of fraudulent benefit

---

## Conclusion

ESI provides comprehensive medical and cash benefits to low-wage workers (≤₹21,000/month), covering 13 crore workers across India. Key compliance: Register employer + employees, contribute 4% (0.75% employee + 3.25% employer) by 15th monthly, ensure employees avail free treatment at ESIC facilities. Benefits include lifelong medical care (family included), sickness benefit (70% wages × 91 days), maternity (100% wages × 182 days), disability pension, and dependents benefit. Low contribution cost (₹720/month for ₹18K salary) delivers significant value (lakhs in medical coverage annually). Strict enforcement (imprisonment for non-registration, heavy interest/damages for delays) ensures compliance.
