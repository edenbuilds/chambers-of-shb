# Protocol: Pre-Grant Opposition

**Protocol ID**: PROT-IPL-006
**Version**: 1.0.0
**Domain**: Indian Patent Law
**Category**: Opposition Proceedings
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-16

---

## Purpose

Comprehensive guide for filing and prosecuting pre-grant oppositions under Section 25(1) of the Patents Act, 1970, including the significant changes from the 2024 Amendment Rules introducing prima facie review.

---

## Activation Triggers

- Competitor patent application threatens business
- Prior art discovered that invalidates pending application
- Public interest grounds for opposing patent grant
- Defensive patent strategy

---

## Prerequisites

- Published patent application (post 18 months or early publication)
- Identified grounds for opposition under Section 25(1)
- Evidence supporting opposition grounds
- Application not yet granted

---

## Core Process

### Step 1: Verify Application Status

Before filing opposition:
1. Check application is **published** (search on InPASS)
2. Confirm application is **not yet granted**
3. Verify examination report issued (timing consideration)

**Timeline Window**: After publication, before grant

### Step 2: Identify Opposition Grounds (Section 25(1))

| Ground | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| (a) | Wrongfully obtained from opponent or predecessor |
| (b) | Published before priority date (anticipation) |
| (c) | Publicly known/used in India before priority date |
| (d) | Obvious in view of prior art |
| (e) | Not an invention under the Act |
| (f) | Non-patentable under Section 3 |
| (g) | Insufficient description |
| (h) | Non-disclosure of information under Section 8 |
| (i) | Convention application filed after 12 months |
| (j) | Not entitled to claim priority date |
| (k) | Claims not fairly based on matter disclosed |

### Step 3: Prepare Opposition Representation

#### Required Components:

1. **Written Statement of Opposition**
   - Detailed grounds with specificity
   - Reference to each claim opposed
   - Prima facie case establishment

2. **Evidence in Support**
   - Prior art documents
   - Expert declarations (if applicable)
   - Proof of prior use/knowledge
   - Section 8 non-compliance evidence

3. **Form and Fee**
   - Pre-grant opposition fee (2024 Rules):
     - ₹20,000 (all entities)
   - No prescribed form number

### Step 4: Prima Facie Review (NEW - 2024 Rules)

#### Rule 55(3) Requirement:
Controller must first assess whether representation discloses a **prima facie case** for opposition.

#### If Prima Facie Case Found:
1. Opposition accepted on record
2. Applicant notified
3. Application proceeds under **expedited examination** (Rule 24C)
4. Full opposition procedure initiated

#### If Prima Facie Case NOT Found:
1. Notice of rejection issued under Rule 55(3)
2. Opponent may request hearing
3. If no hearing requested, opposition dismissed
4. **Reasoned order** must be provided for refusal

### Step 5: Opposition Procedure (Post-Acceptance)

#### Timeline After Prima Facie Acceptance:

| Event | Timeline |
|-------|----------|
| Notice to applicant | Controller issues notice |
| Applicant's reply | Within prescribed period |
| Evidence submission | As directed by Controller |
| Hearing (if requested) | Scheduled by Controller |
| Controller's decision | After hearing/submissions |

### Step 6: Hearing Procedures

Per 2024 Rules, hearing procedures from post-grant opposition (Rules 62(2)-(4)) now apply to pre-grant:

1. **Notice of Hearing**: Issued to both parties
2. **Hearing Fee**: ₹7,500 (must be paid to attend)
3. **Written Submissions**: Filed before hearing
4. **Oral Arguments**: Both parties may present
5. **Decision**: Controller issues reasoned order

### Step 7: Possible Outcomes

1. **Opposition Succeeds**: Application refused
2. **Opposition Partially Succeeds**: Claims amended/deleted
3. **Opposition Fails**: Application proceeds to grant
4. **Opposition Dismissed at Prima Facie**: No further action

---

## 2024 Rule Changes Summary

| Aspect | Before 2024 | After 2024 |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| Prima Facie Filter | None | Mandatory under Rule 55(3) |
| Fee | None | ₹20,000 |
| Hearing Fee | None | ₹7,500 |
| Expedited Examination | No | Yes, if opposition accepted |
| Reasoned Order | Not required | Required for prima facie refusal |

## Strategic Considerations

### When to File:
- **Early**: Maximum time to gather evidence
- **After FER**: Leverage examiner's objections
- **Before grant imminent**: Last opportunity

### Evidence Strength:
- Strong documentary prior art preferred
- Expert declarations add weight
- Section 8 grounds increasingly successful

### Cost-Benefit Analysis:
- Filing fee: ₹20,000
- Hearing fee: ₹7,500 (if attending)
- Attorney fees
- Time investment
- vs. Risk of competitor patent grant

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Application verified as published, not granted
- [ ] Opposition grounds clearly identified
- [ ] Prima facie case articulated
- [ ] Prior art properly cited with dates
- [ ] Claims specifically addressed
- [ ] Evidence organized and referenced
- [ ] Fee paid
- [ ] Timeline tracked for responses

---

## Common Opposition Mistakes

1. **Filing after grant**: Must use post-grant opposition
2. **Weak prima facie case**: Rejected at threshold
3. **Generic arguments**: Lack specificity required
4. **Missing evidence**: Unsupported allegations
5. **Not paying hearing fee**: Cannot attend hearing

## Opposition Statement Template

```
REPRESENTATION FOR OPPOSITION UNDER SECTION 25(1)

Application No.: [Number]
Title: [Title of Invention]
Applicant: [Name]

Opponent: [Name and Address]

GROUNDS OF OPPOSITION:

1. [Ground (b) - Anticipation]
   The claims 1-5 are anticipated by [prior art document] published
   on [date], which discloses [specific features]...

2. [Ground (d) - Obviousness]
   Claims 6-10 lack inventive step in view of combination of
   [Document A] and [Document B]...

3. [Ground (h) - Section 8 Non-Compliance]
   The applicant failed to disclose [foreign application/prosecution
   history] as required under Section 8...

PRIMA FACIE CASE:
[Summary establishing threshold case for opposition]

RELIEF SOUGHT:
Refusal of patent application / Amendment of claims [specify]

[Attachments: Prior art documents, declarations, evidence]
```
