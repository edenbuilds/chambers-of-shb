# Protocol: Technology Transfer Agreements

**Protocol ID**: PROT-IPL-037
**Version**: 1.0.0
**Domain**: Indian Patent Law
**Category**: Commercialization
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-16

---

## Purpose

Comprehensive guide for technology transfer agreements in India, covering regulatory requirements, FEMA compliance, RBI approval processes, transfer pricing considerations, and agreement structuring.

---

## Activation Triggers

- Technology import from foreign party
- Technology export to foreign entity
- Joint venture with technology component
- Franchise arrangements involving IP
- R&D collaboration with technology sharing

---

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of FEMA regulations
- Knowledge of RBI automatic/approval routes
- Transfer pricing awareness
- Technology description and valuation

---

## Core Process

### Step 1: Types of Technology Transfer

#### Technology Transfer Categories:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Know-how license** | Technical information, trade secrets |
| **Patent license** | Rights under patents |
| **Technical assistance** | Services, training, support |
| **Franchise** | Business system + IP |
| **Joint development** | Collaborative R&D |
| **Manufacturing license** | Production rights + IP |

### Step 2: Regulatory Framework

#### Key Regulations:

| Regulation | Applicability |
|------------|---------------|
| **FEMA** | Foreign exchange, cross-border |
| **RBI guidelines** | Approval/automatic route |
| **Transfer pricing** | Arm's length pricing |
| **Competition Act** | Anti-competitive terms |
| **Patents Act** | Patent-specific terms |

#### FEMA - Foreign Exchange Management Act:

- Governs foreign exchange transactions
- Technology payments to non-residents
- Prior approval/automatic route

### Step 3: RBI Automatic vs. Approval Route

#### Automatic Route (Most Technology Transfers):

**Eligible Payments**:
- Lump sum: Up to USD 2 million
- Royalty: Up to 5% of domestic sales, 8% of exports
- Technical services: Up to 2% of export, 1% of domestic

**Conditions**:
- Arm's length pricing
- Proper documentation
- AD Bank reporting

#### Approval Route:

**Required When**:
- Payments exceed automatic limits
- Related party transactions
- Specific restricted sectors
- Unusual payment structures

**Process**:
```
Application to RBI
        ↓
Documentation submission
        ↓
RBI review (60-90 days typical)
        ↓
Approval with conditions
        ↓
Payment permitted
```

### Step 4: Agreement Structure

#### Key Components:

```
TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER AND LICENSE AGREEMENT

1. PARTIES
   Transferor: [Foreign company details]
   Transferee: [Indian company details]

2. RECITALS
   - Transferor owns/controls technology
   - Transferee wishes to acquire rights
   - Regulatory compliance intended

3. DEFINITIONS
   - "Technology": [Detailed definition]
   - "Know-How": [Technical information]
   - "Licensed IP": [Patents, trademarks]
   - "Products": [What can be manufactured]
   - "Territory": [India unless otherwise]

4. GRANT OF RIGHTS
   - License under patents
   - Know-how communication
   - Technical assistance
   - Manufacturing rights

5. TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE
   - Training programs
   - Technical visits
   - Documentation supply
   - Ongoing support

6. CONSIDERATION
   - Lump sum: USD [Amount]
   - Running royalty: [X]% of Net Sales
   - Technical service fee: [Amount/rate]
   - Payment schedule: [Timeline]

7. DELIVERY OF TECHNOLOGY
   - Documentation delivery: [Schedule]
   - Training: [Schedule]
   - Technical visits: [Number, duration]
   - Updates: [Ongoing/limited]

8. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
   - FEMA compliance
   - RBI filings
   - Transfer pricing documentation
   - Tax withholding

9. CONFIDENTIALITY
   - Obligations of confidentiality
   - Permitted disclosures
   - Duration of obligation

10. TERM AND TERMINATION
    - Initial term: [X] years
    - Renewal: [Mechanism]
    - Termination for cause
    - Survival of provisions

11. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTES
    - Governing law: [...]
    - Arbitration: [Seat, rules]
```

### Step 5: Transfer Pricing Compliance

#### Arm's Length Requirement:

**Section 92 - Income Tax Act**:
Related party transactions must be at arm's length price.

#### Transfer Pricing Methods:

| Method | Use |
|--------|-----|
| **CUP** (Comparable Uncontrolled Price) | Direct comparables available |
| **RPM** (Resale Price Method) | Distribution arrangements |
| **CPM** (Cost Plus Method) | Manufacturing/services |
| **PSM** (Profit Split Method) | Integrated operations |
| **TNMM** (Transactional Net Margin) | When others not applicable |

#### Documentation Requirements:

- Master file (global operations)
- Local file (Indian entity)
- Country-by-country report (if applicable)
- Contemporaneous documentation

### Step 6: Tax Implications

#### Withholding Tax (TDS):

| Payment Type | Rate (With DTAA) | Rate (Without DTAA) |
|--------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Royalty | 10-15% (varies by treaty) | 20% |
| Technical fees | 10-15% (varies by treaty) | 20% |
| Lump sum | Treaty rate or domestic | 20% |

#### GST on Services:

- Import of services: Reverse charge mechanism
- GST rate: 18% typically
- Input credit available

#### Permanent Establishment Risk:

Technology transfer may create PE if:
- Transferor employees in India > 90 days
- Decision-making authority in India
- Fixed place of business created

### Step 7: Competition Law Considerations

#### Potentially Anti-Competitive Terms:

| Clause | Risk |
|--------|------|
| **Exclusive grant-back** | May be anti-competitive |
| **Tying arrangements** | Problematic |
| **No-challenge clause** | Potentially void |
| **Post-term restrictions** | May be excessive |
| **Price fixing** | Anti-competitive |
| **Territory restrictions** | Assess reasonableness |

#### Safe Provisions:

- Non-exclusive grant-back
- Quality control requirements
- Confidentiality restrictions
- Field of use limitations (reasonable)

### Step 8: Documentation and Filing

#### AD Bank Filings:

**Form FC-GPR**: Foreign collaboration filing
**Form FC-TRS**: Technology transfer details

#### Required Documents:

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Agreement | Primary document |
| Board resolution | Authority to enter |
| Valuation certificate | Support arm's length |
| CA certificate | Compliance certification |
| RBI approval | If approval route |

## Common Technology Transfer Mistakes

1. **Exceeding automatic limits**: Need approval route
2. **No transfer pricing documentation**: Assessment risk
3. **Missing withholding tax**: Liability + interest
4. **Anti-competitive terms**: CCI risk
5. **PE creation**: Unintended tax presence
6. **Inadequate technology description**: Disputes
