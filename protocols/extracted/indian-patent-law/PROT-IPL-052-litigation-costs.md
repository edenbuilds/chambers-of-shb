# Protocol: Patent Litigation Costs and Fee Recovery

**Protocol ID**: PROT-IPL-052
**Version**: 1.0.0
**Domain**: Indian Patent Law
**Category**: Litigation
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-16

---

## Purpose

Comprehensive guide to understanding, estimating, and managing patent litigation costs in India, including court fees, attorney fees, expert costs, and potential fee recovery mechanisms.

---

## Activation Triggers

- Planning patent litigation
- Budgeting for enforcement
- Evaluating litigation risk
- Seeking cost recovery
- Defending infringement claims

---

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of litigation stages
- Knowledge of court fee structures
- Awareness of attorney fee practices
- Familiarity with cost allocation rules

## Core Process

### Step 1: Categories of Litigation Costs

#### Cost Components:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Court fees** | Fees paid to court |
| **Attorney fees** | Legal representation |
| **Expert fees** | Technical experts |
| **Discovery costs** | Evidence gathering |
| **Administrative** | Filing, service, etc. |
| **Travel** | Appearances, meetings |

### Step 2: Court Fees

#### Commercial Court Fees:

```
COMMERCIAL COURT FEE STRUCTURE

Delhi High Court (Commercial):
├── Ad valorem fee on suit value
├── Fixed fees for interlocutory applications
└── Document registration fees

Typical Calculation:
├── Suit valuation: As per plaint
├── Court fee: Percentage of valuation
│   ├── Up to Rs. 1 crore: [Rate]
│   └── Above Rs. 1 crore: [Tiered]
└── Application fees: Fixed amounts
```

#### Fee Types:

| Type | Basis |
|------|-------|
| **Plaint fee** | Suit valuation |
| **Application fee** | Per application |
| **Appeal fee** | Decree value |
| **Caveat fee** | Fixed |
| **Certified copy** | Per page |

### Step 3: Attorney Fees

#### Fee Structures:

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Hourly** | Per hour worked |
| **Fixed/Flat** | Per task or stage |
| **Success fee** | Contingent on outcome |
| **Retainer** | Monthly ongoing |
| **Hybrid** | Combination |

#### Typical Fee Ranges:

```
ATTORNEY FEE ESTIMATES (2024-2026)

Senior Counsel (Designation):
├── Appearance: Rs. 5-50 lakhs+ per hearing
├── Brief: Additional fees
└── Conference: Per hour

Law Firm Partner:
├── Hourly: Rs. 25,000 - 75,000+
├── Per matter: Varies by complexity

Associate Level:
├── Hourly: Rs. 5,000 - 25,000
├── Research/drafting: Included in bill

Note: Rates vary significantly by:
├── Court (Delhi HC vs. others)
├── Complexity
├── Firm reputation
└── Duration
```

### Step 6: Total Litigation Cost Estimates

#### By Case Type:

```
PATENT LITIGATION COST ESTIMATES

1. Interim Injunction Only:
   ├── Court fees: Rs. 1-5 lakhs
   ├── Attorney: Rs. 10-50 lakhs
   ├── Expert: Rs. 5-15 lakhs
   └── Total: Rs. 20-75 lakhs

2. Full Trial (1-2 years):
   ├── Court fees: Rs. 5-15 lakhs
   ├── Attorney: Rs. 50-200+ lakhs
   ├── Expert: Rs. 20-50 lakhs
   ├── Discovery: Rs. 10-30 lakhs
   └── Total: Rs. 1-5 crores

3. Complex Multi-Patent:
   ├── Court fees: Rs. 10-25 lakhs
   ├── Attorney: Rs. 1-5+ crores
   ├── Expert: Rs. 50-150 lakhs
   ├── Discovery: Rs. 25-100 lakhs
   └── Total: Rs. 3-10+ crores
```

### Step 7: Cost Recovery (Section 35 CPC)

#### General Rule:

Costs follow the event - successful party may recover costs.

#### Recoverable Costs:

| Cost Type | Recoverable? |
|-----------|-------------|
| Court fees | Yes |
| Attorney fees | Limited/discretionary |
| Expert fees | Discretionary |
| Discovery costs | Rarely |
| Travel | Rarely |

#### Cost Recovery Framework:

```
COST RECOVERY ANALYSIS

Court Discretion Factors:
├── Outcome of litigation
├── Conduct of parties
├── Reasonableness of claims/defenses
├── Settlement offers rejected
└── Delay tactics

Typical Recovery:
├── Court fees: Usually recoverable
├── Legal fees: Nominal award common
├── Actual costs: Rarely full recovery
└── Punitive costs: Exceptional cases

Reality:
├── Full cost recovery: Rare
├── Partial recovery: Common
├── No recovery: Not unusual
└── Each party bears own: Typical order
```

## Common Cost Mistakes

1. **Underestimating duration**: Cases take longer
2. **No contingency**: Unexpected costs
3. **Expert over-reliance**: Excessive expert fees
4. **Poor vendor selection**: Overpaying for services
5. **No early assessment**: Surprises late
6. **Ignoring settlement**: Fighting unwinnable cases
