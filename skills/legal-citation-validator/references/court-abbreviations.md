# Court Abbreviations - Indian Legal Citations

## Supreme Court

**Supreme Court of India**:
- Abbreviation: **SC**
- Usage: `AIR [Year] SC [Page]`
- Example: `AIR 1973 SC 1461`

**Alternative Names**:
- Supreme Court of India
- Apex Court (informal, not in citations)
- Hon'ble Supreme Court (in formal addresses)

## High Courts (25 Courts)

- Abbreviation: **All**
- State/UT: Uttar Pradesh
- Bench: Lucknow bench also exists
- Example: `AIR 2020 All 145`

## District Courts

**General Format**:
District courts not typically cited in AIR. Use court name:
```
[Party] v. [Party], [Judgment Date], [Court Name]
```

Example:
- Ram v. Shyam, 15.03.2020, District Court Delhi (Tis Hazari)

## Tribunals

### National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)
- Abbreviation: **NCLT**
- Benches: Multiple (Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, etc.)
- Example: `[Company] v. [Company], [Year] NCLT [Bench] [Number]`

### National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT)
- Abbreviation: **NCLAT**
- Location: New Delhi
- Example: `Company Appeal (AT) No. [X]/[Year]`

### National Green Tribunal (NGT)
- Abbreviation: **NGT**
- Benches: Principal (Delhi), Regional benches
- Example: `Original Application No. [X]/[Year] (NGT)`

### Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT)
- Abbreviation: **CAT**
- Multiple benches across India
- Example: `OA No. [X]/[Year] (CAT [Bench])`

### Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT)
- Abbreviation: **ITAT**
- Benches: Multiple cities
- Example: `ITA No. [X]/[Year] (ITAT [City])`

### Customs, Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal (CESTAT)
- Abbreviation: **CESTAT**
- Benches: Multiple cities
- Example: `Appeal No. [X]/[Year] (CESTAT [City])`

### Armed Forces Tribunal (AFT)
- Abbreviation: **AFT**
- Regional benches
- Example: `TA No. [X]/[Year] (AFT [Bench])`

### Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT)
- Abbreviation: **DRT**
- Multiple benches
- Example: `SA No. [X]/[Year] (DRT [City])`

### Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunal (DRAT)
- Abbreviation: **DRAT**
- Appellate authority
- Example: `Appeal No. [X]/[Year] (DRAT [City])`

## Historical Courts (Pre-Independence)

### Privy Council
- Abbreviation: **PC**
- Citations rare in modern practice
- Example: `[Case], [Year] PC [Page]`

### Federal Court of India (1937-1950)
- Abbreviation: **FC** or **FCR**
- Dissolved upon Supreme Court establishment
- Example: `[Case], [Year] FCR [Page]`

## Citation Format Summary

**Supreme Court**:
```
AIR [Year] SC [Page]
```

**High Courts**:
```
AIR [Year] [HC Abbreviation] [Page]
```

**Tribunals** (varies by tribunal):
```
[Case/Application No.] ([Tribunal Abbreviation] [Bench if applicable])
```

## Common Errors

### Incorrect Abbreviations
- ❌ S.C. → ✅ SC
- ❌ B.H.C. → ✅ Bom
- ❌ D.H.C. → ✅ Del
- ❌ HC → ✅ [Specific HC abbreviation]

### Ambiguous Citations
- ❌ "High Court" → ✅ Specify which HC
- ❌ "District Court" → ✅ Specify location
- ❌ "Tribunal" → ✅ Specify which tribunal

### Spacing Errors
- ❌ "AIR2020Del189" → ✅ "AIR 2020 Del 189"
- ❌ "AIR 2020Del 189" → ✅ "AIR 2020 Del 189"
