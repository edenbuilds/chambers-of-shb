# Landmark Cases Index - Verified Citations

## Constitutional Law

### Basic Structure Doctrine
- **Kesavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala**, AIR 1973 SC 1461 (13-judge bench)
- **Minerva Mills v. Union of India**, AIR 1980 SC 1789

### Article 14 - Equality
- **E.P. Royappa v. State of Tamil Nadu**, AIR 1974 SC 555 (introduced "arbitrariness" test)
- **Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India**, AIR 1978 SC 597 (expanded Article 21)

### Article 21 - Life & Liberty
- **Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India**, AIR 1978 SC 597 (procedure must be fair & reasonable)
- **Francis Coralie Mullin v. UT of Delhi**, AIR 1981 SC 746 (right to live with dignity)
- **Vishaka v. State of Rajasthan**, AIR 1997 SC 3011 (sexual harassment guidelines)

### Judicial Review
- **L. Chandra Kumar v. Union of India**, AIR 1997 SC 1125 (tribunal decisions subject to HC writ)
