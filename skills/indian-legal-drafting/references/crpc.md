# Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC), 1973

## Purpose
The CrPC governs the procedure for administration of criminal law in India.

## Key Sections

### Section 154 – FIR
- Mandatory registration for cognizable offences

### Section 156(3) – Magistrate Investigation Power
- Used when police fail to act

### Section 190 – Cognizance of Offences  

### Section 200 – Examination of Complainant  

### Section 202 – Inquiry / Investigation  

### Section 204 – Issue of Process  

### Section 438 – Anticipatory Bail  

### Section 439 – Regular Bail  

## Practical Use in Drafting
- Use 156(3) when FIR is not registered  
- Use 200 for private complaints
