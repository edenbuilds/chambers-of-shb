# Contract Advice Command

**Task**: Provide comprehensive legal advice on contract-related issues under Indian law.

## Workflow

1. **Gather Context**:
   - Contract type (general, sale, guarantee, bailment, agency, partnership)
   - Stage (pre-formation, formation, performance, breach, dispute)
   - Specific issue (validity, performance, breach, rights, remedies)
   - Key facts (parties, subject matter, consideration, dates, status)

2. **Load Relevant Protocols**:
   - Formation: IL-CONTRACT-FORMATION, IL-CONTRACT-CAPACITY, IL-CONTRACT-FREE-CONSENT, IL-CONTRACT-LEGALITY
   - Performance: IL-CONTRACT-PERFORMANCE, IL-CONTRACT-DISCHARGE, IL-CONTRACT-IMPOSSIBILITY
   - Breach/Remedies: IL-CONTRACT-BREACH, IL-CONTRACT-DAMAGES, IL-CONTRACT-SPECIFIC-PERFORMANCE, IL-CONTRACT-INJUNCTION, IL-CONTRACT-RESCISSION
   - Special Contracts: IL-CONTRACT-SALE-OF-GOODS, IL-CONTRACT-INDEMNITY-GUARANTEE, IL-CONTRACT-BAILMENT-PLEDGE, IL-CONTRACT-AGENCY, IL-CONTRACT-PARTNERSHIP

3. **Analyze**:
   - **Validity**: Section 10 elements check → Valid/Voidable/Void determination
   - **Performance**: Obligations (who, when, where, how) + discharge methods
   - **Breach**: Classification (actual/anticipatory, material/non-material) + remedies
   - Apply statutory sections + landmark cases

4. **Provide Advice**:
   - Legal analysis (statutory basis + case law)
   - Rights and obligations (yours + other party's)
   - Remedies available (primary + alternatives with viability)
   - Risks and defenses
   - Recommendations (immediate actions + strategy)
   - Evidence required + timeline (limitation period)

**Use contract-law-specialist or contract-remedies-expert agents for deeper analysis.**
