# Slash Command: /resolve-labour-dispute - Comprehensive Labour Dispute Resolution Strategy

## Purpose
Analyze employment termination, wrongful discharge, or statutory benefits dispute, assess domestic enquiry compliance, calculate all statutory dues (PF/ESI/Gratuity/Bonus), evaluate reinstatement prospects, and provide complete dispute resolution strategy from settlement to labor court litigation.

## Usage
```
/resolve-labour-dispute <dispute-description>
```

## What This Command Does

When you invoke `/resolve-labour-dispute`, I will systematically:

1. **Employment Status Assessment:**
   - Determine workman vs non-workman status (wage threshold < Rs. 10,000/month)
   - Calculate continuous service period (for statutory benefits eligibility)
   - Identify applicable laws (ID Act, Factories Act, Shops & Establishments Act)

2. **Statutory Benefits Calculation:**
   - **PF (Provident Fund)**: Employee + Employer contributions (12% each on Basic + DA)
   - **ESI (Employee State Insurance)**: 0.75% employee + 3.25% employer (if wage ≤ Rs. 21,000)
   - **Gratuity**: (Last drawn salary × 15 days × Years) / 26 (if 5+ years service)
   - **Bonus**: 8.33% minimum (if wage ≤ Rs. 21,000, establishment 20+ employees)
   - **Leave Encashment**: Earned leave balance × Daily wage
   - **Notice Pay**: 1 month (if termination without notice)
   - **Retrenchment Compensation**: 15 days average pay × Years (if retrenchment)

3. **Domestic Enquiry Compliance Audit:**
   - Verify charge sheet adequacy (specific allegations, reasonable time to respond)
   - Assess enquiry officer independence (not biased/interested party)
   - Check opportunity to defend (witness examination, cross-examination rights)
   - Evaluate findings (evidence-based, not assumptions)
   - Determine procedural justice compliance (natural justice principles)

4. **Section 25F Retrenchment Compliance (If Applicable):**
   - Notice: 1 month OR notice pay ✓/✗
   - Retrenchment Compensation: 15 days × Years ✓/✗
   - Government Approval: If 100+ workers ✓/✗
   - Compliance Status: VALID / INVALID

5. **Legal Remedies Analysis:**
   - Reinstatement + Back Wages (if wrongful termination)
   - Compensation in Lieu (typically 3-5 months wages × years)
   - Statutory Dues Recovery
   - Success Probability Assessment (based on domestic enquiry defects)

6. **Dispute Resolution Strategy:**
   - **Phase 1**: Pre-litigation settlement negotiation (15-30 days)
   - **Phase 2**: Conciliation (Labour Commissioner - 21 days)
   - **Phase 3**: Labour Court adjudication (Section 10A - 6-18 months)
   - **Phase 4**: High Court writ (Alternative for statutory violations - 12-24 months)

7. **Cost-Benefit Analysis:**
   - Litigation costs (advocate fees, court fees, timeline)
   - Expected recovery (best/likely/worst case scenarios)
   - Settlement vs litigation trade-off recommendation

8. **Tactical Guidance:**
   - Settlement negotiation leverage points
   - Evidence gathering (documents, witnesses)
   - Conciliation strategy (first settlement attempt)
   - Labor court litigation preparation

## Example Usage

I was terminated on 1st Oct 2024 for "misconduct" but the company did not give me any charge sheet or allow me to explain. They just asked me to leave immediately. No notice pay or severance paid.
